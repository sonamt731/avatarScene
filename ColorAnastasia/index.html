Color Tracking Painting Game

This is a color tracking game. You can create your own Pandora picture here.
Starting by assigning colors to stickers. You can do this easily by clicking on the color you like.
You may want to use distincly different colors to make your painting easier.
After assigning all the colors, you'll enter the painting mode.
Show the color at anywhere you want to put corresponding stickers, then press the SPACE button, the stikcer will appear.
use A and D to adjust the accuracy.
